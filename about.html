About us

About us

Gastronilo

The purpose of this idea is to propose a new approach to overcome the difficulty of introducing food gastronomy by providing an application that is made to provide information related to food gastronomy with detailed information on the typical food of the island of Lombok and include cultural information behind the existence of the food.

Our Program

Our Program

Build a responsive website that is easy to access and comfortable for users to use. Provide information regarding names, descriptions, images, culture and the history of the typical food of the island of lombok. Provide information related to the story behind the taste and type of food typical of the island of lombok. Provide information related to the nutrients contained, recipes and health. Chatbot to increase interaction from the website to the user.

Taliwang Chicken, Lombok's Special Food, Boost Your Taste

Taliwang chicken is a special food originating from Taliwang, West Sumbawa. This food was first popularized outside the region by the Taliwang people who live in Taliwang Village, Mataram City, West Nusa Tenggara. Many misinterpret that this Taliwang chicken is a typical Lombok food. Even though this food is a typical food from Taliwang, West Sumbawa, which was first popularized on the island of Lombok. This dish is made from chicken. The chicken meat served comes from young free-range chicken which is grilled and then seasoned with a kind of sauce whose ingredients include dry red chilies, shallots, garlic, tomatoes, fried shrimp paste, kencur, brown sugar, and salt. This food is usually served with other Lombok specialties, such as plecing kale.

Ayam Taliwang, Makanan Khas dari Nusa Tenggara Barat

Ayam Taliwang biasanya disajikan Bersama nasi dan lalapan seperti tomat dan mentimun. Sambal bajak khas taliwang juga akan menjadi pelengkap tersendiri untuk menghidangkan makanan khas Nusa Tenggar Barat ini. Konon kabarnya, Ayam Taliwang awalnya menjadi makanan prajurit Taliwang saat bertempur. Bumbu racikan seadanya dan acar masak sederhana memang merupakan ciri khas cara masak prajurit. Masakan ini kemudian dipopulerkan oleh Presiden Soekarno saat pertama kali berkunjung ke Taliwang, Nusa Tenggara Barat pada tahun 1960.
